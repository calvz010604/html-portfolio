The Best Movies according to Calvin

My Top 5 Movies.

1. Ready Player One

“Ready Player One” is at once familiar in its fabric and forward-thinking in its technology, with a combination of gritty live action and glossy CGI. It’s an ambitious mix that can be thrilling while it lasts, and yet it fails to linger for long afterward, leaving you wondering what its point is beyond validating the insularity of ravenous fandom (Lemire, 2018).

2. Spider Man

Just like Peter Parker, I can also be your friendly neighborhood, haha!.

3. Harry Potter (all movies)

Feels like I want to study at Hogwarts and meet the Hermoine of my life.

4. Pacific Rim & Pacific Rim: Uprising

Jaegers versus Kaijus! such a cool movie!

5. Spy Kids movies

Intelligent and talented kids to motivate your little brothers and sisters!
